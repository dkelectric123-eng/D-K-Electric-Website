Our Story

Meet Dany

D&K Electric was started by Dany, a licensed electrician with over 20+ years of experience. In business for 3 years. He started D&K Electric because he believes every job should be done right — no shortcuts, no excuses.

Dany is C10 certified, fully licensed in Illinois, and insured on every job. He serves homeowners, contractors, and businesses across Kane County and DuPage County.

When you call D&K Electric, you talk to Dany. Not a call center. He will come out, look at the job, give you a straight price, and do the work himself. That's how it works at D&K Electric.

D&K Electric is a family owned and operated business — and we treat every job like it is our own home.
